# Lawrence Piano Studio Website Design

## 1. Overview and Purpose
A professional, elegant, and welcoming website for Lawrence Piano Studio. The site serves as the primary marketing and informational hub for prospective students and parents, providing clear details on instruction, pricing, and contact methods, alongside a blog for resources.

## 2. Architecture & Stack
- **Framework:** Astro (Static Site Generator)
- **Theme/Styling:** Astrowind (Tailwind CSS integration)
- **Structure:** Multi-page architecture (Home, About, Lessons/Pricing, Blog, Contact)
- **Visual Vibe:** Elegant & Professional (classic typography, refined spacing, timeless feel with black, white, and gold/cream accents).

## 3. Page Structure & Content Breakdown
### Home
- **Hero:** High-quality image (piano keys/hands), elegant headline ("Elevate Your Musical Journey"), CTA ("Start Learning").
- **Value Proposition:** 3 columns highlighting expertise, all-ages approach, and performance opportunities.
- **Social Proof:** Testimonials from students/parents.
- **Footer:** Contact info, location, quick links, social links.

### About
- **Instructor Bio:** Professional headshot, background, and credentials.
- **Teaching Philosophy:** Approach to music education.

### Lessons & Pricing
- **Program Details:** Breakdown of lesson lengths (30, 45, 60 mins).
- **Pricing Table:** Clear, transparent pricing.
- **Policies:** Cancellations, makeups.

### Blog
- **Grid View:** Recent articles, practice tips, studio news.

### Contact
- **Form:** Name, Email, Phone, Message, Student Age/Level dropdown.
- **Direct Info:** Email, Phone, Location map.

## 4. Development Workflow
- Rapid scaffolding using Astro + Astrowind.
- Iterative design refinement with live local preview for the user.
- Content injection as provided by the client.
