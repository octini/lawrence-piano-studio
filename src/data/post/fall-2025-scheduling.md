We are starting work on the fall schedule, which begins **Sunday, August 24**! Check your emails for more details, but here's the short version:

- Returning private students will get an email/call from their teacher during the week of August 7th. We will do our best to let students retain their lesson time from last year if desired.
- Returning hour-long group students will be contacted beginning August 1.
- We will schedule new students & the returning K–1st group students beginning August 11.

With the return to the school year we also return to monthly billing. Students receive 34 lessons during the school year and that cost is distributed evenly so students are charged the same every month. There's no additional fee for practice recitals and recitals.

Eric will be working on the schedule every day so don't hesitate to contact him.

Enjoy the rest of the summer!
