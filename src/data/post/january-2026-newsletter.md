Piano lessons for all students in group and private lessons will begin again the week of **January 5, 2026**.

All lesson times will remain the same as December unless we have discussed a change. Please let us know ASAP if you need to change your lesson time. We will be making adjustments to the schedule as well as scheduling new students before we begin January 5th.

## Group Studio Recitals

All students in **hour-long group lessons** will have a **studio recital the week of March 30th**, two weeks after Lawrence Public Schools' spring break. This recital will be at the studio during your regular lesson time.

## Spring Recitals

We will be having our Spring Recitals at the **Lawrence Arts Center** for all students **(both group and private).**

These recitals are scheduled for **Monday, May 18th – Thursday May 21st**.

Recital sign-up information will be sent at the beginning of April.

Have a great 2026!

— Eric
